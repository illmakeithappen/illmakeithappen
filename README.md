## About

I work independently on the design and evaluation of intelligent systems, with
a background in economics, quantitative policy research, causal inference, and
applied AI engineering.

My earlier work sits in international trade, investment policy, and the
transformation of economic and social systems. Today I am interested in how AI
expands human potential while reshaping work unevenly across institutions,
markets, and regions.

I write about the more academic side of this on
[gitthub labs](https://labs.gitthub.org). At
[Wirenet](https://wirenet.dev), I work on the operating layer around creative
and knowledge work: agent-readable context, reusable workflows, review loops,
and systems that let agents work with people more effectively than a chat
window can.

## Current Work

- **AI work systems** - agentic workspaces, coding-agent workflows, review

- **Applied research tooling** - causal inference, trade-flow analysis,
  sanctions pathways, supply-chain dynamics, and data products for policy work.
- **Writing and experiments** - essays and prototypes at
  [gitthub labs](https://labs.gitthub.org), plus public code here on GitHub.
